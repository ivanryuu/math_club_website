The Introduction

The UT Math club is all about ...

enhancing the undergraduate math experience by exposing students to beautiful mathematics

The Undergraduate Mathematics Research Club, or Math Club, is a student group sponsored by the UT-Austin Mathematics Department and formed under the auspices of the Geometry Group RTG grant from the National Science Foundation.

The Details

"Pure mathematics is, in its way, the poetry of logical ideas"

-Albert Einstein

Who can join

It is open to all students interested in mathematics, and especially caters to the UT-Austin pure and applied mathematics communities.

When are the meetings

Meetings are every Wednesday from 5-7pm on the 12th floor lounge in RLM, 12.104

Who will talk

Speakers include UT faculty, graduate students, and undergraduates, as well as researchers visiting as part of the RTG program.

What else is offered

We also hold information sessions on topics like applying to graduate school, things to do over the summer, and how to use the computer resources in the math department.
